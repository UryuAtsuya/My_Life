# Short Video Operations OS

## Hypothesis

Use Codex and company to manage short-video ideation, scripting, production, posting, and review. First validate the workflow through personal content production, then package it as either monthly short-video production/operations or a workflow product.

## Why This First

This path is closer to revenue than a new SaaS, Daily Planner, job-hunting site, or Pinterest-style AI workflow. Each produced video becomes both a portfolio artifact and evidence that the operating process works.

## Two-Week Validation

- Produce 10 short videos with Remotion
- Focus themes around AI usage, Codex usage, and solo-business automation
- Manage the process in company
- Create a one-page service menu
- Pitch 10 people

## Success Criteria

- 10 videos produced
- 3 people show interest
- 1 person enters a paid or paid-intent trial
