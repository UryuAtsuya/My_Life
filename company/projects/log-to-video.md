# Log To Video

## Goal

Show one concrete transformation: a Codex run log becomes a 15-second vertical video.

## Working Hook

その作業ログ、投稿にできます。

## Scope

- One input: a single Codex run log or short implementation record.
- One output: a 15-second vertical short.
- One proof moment: before/after from raw log to publishable clip.
- One CTA: invite people to send an AI/business log for the same treatment.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The demo can be understood without explaining the whole operations system.
- It shows the source log and the resulting video clearly.
- It stays a proof clip, not a product build.
- It has a publishable thumbnail and one caption.
