# Morning Company Automation

## Purpose

Run a daily company morning workflow with Codex automation.

## Flow

1. Create or update today's TODO
2. Choose discussion topics from that TODO
3. Run a business meeting with PM, creative, engineering, and business/growth viewpoints
4. Save the meeting summary, decisions, and updated TODOs
5. Sync GitHub-worthy notes into `My_Life/company/`
6. Commit and push My_Life changes

## Automation

- ID: `morning-company-business-meeting`
- Name: Morning company business meeting
- Time: every morning at 8:00
- Status: ACTIVE
